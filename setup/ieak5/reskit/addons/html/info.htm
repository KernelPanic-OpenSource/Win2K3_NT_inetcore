Microsoft NetMeeting

NetMeeting enables you to make audio and video calls and share data over the Internet or Intranet. Using NetMeeting, two or more people can share applications, transfer files, view and draw on a shared whiteboard, and chat. All of this will work over a standard modem connection to the Internet, as well as over ISDN and on a local area network.
